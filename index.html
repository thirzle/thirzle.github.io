I am a tenure track assistant professor at the University of Copenhagen in the Human-Centred Computing section of the Department of Computer Science (DIKU). I am also part of the XR Collaboratory of the Pioneer Centre for Artificial Intelligence of the University of Copenhagen. My work is in the field of Human-Computer Interaction with a focus on Extended Reality.
My research interest is currently in the area of Extended Reality for Behavior Change and the intersection of Extended Reality and Artificial Intelligence (check out our 2023 CHI paper on XR and AI for an overview). For an overview of my publications, please go to publications or my Google Scholar profile .
